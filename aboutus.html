We Arrange World’s most Celebrated Events


A one-stop shop for all your needs and requirements when it comes to any kind of events, Solitaire Events is one of the best event management companies around. We are dedicated to supplying our clients with creative solutions, strategic planning and flawless production right from the inception of an event to its execution.

Girish Jain, the founder and director of the company has an experience of more than 8 years in the field of event management. After working as a freelancer with Wizcraft entertainment for a year and partnering with a company for 3 years, Girish founded Solitaire Events with the vision of providing unmatched quality of service that caters to the need and suits the budget of all.

Established in 2014, Solitaire Events has acquired an extensive ambit of clientele in a relatively short period of time. This is because we understand that every event has its own unique purpose and expectations to each of our clients and this understanding forms the foundation on which we build the ideas and solutions that are tailor-made to the clients’ requirements.

Whether it’s a flamboyant grand scale event with a huge guest list or an intimate affair with your close ones that you’re planning, we leave no stone unturned to make it an event to be cherished forever. We are well aware of the fact that it’s not just your money that goes into an event but also your dreams and ambitions and at times even your credibility is at stake, which is why render the best of our services by considering your event as our own.
